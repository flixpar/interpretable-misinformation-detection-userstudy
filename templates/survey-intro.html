Interpretable Misinformation Detection

Thank you for participating in our study!

We are building a framework to help people detect misinformation online. In order to determine how we can best inform users about potential sources of misinformation on social media we are conducting this user study of the tool that we have built.

Your task is to read a series of tweets and rank how likely you think they are to contain misinformation on a 5-point scale. The task will be divided into three parts. First you will have to try to identify misinformation on your own in a normal Twitter feed. Then you will get a new set of tweets, along with corresponding predictions of wheter each tweet contains misinformation. Some of the predictions will be wrong! Finally you will receive a third batch of tweets with predictions and explanations of the predictions, which can help you understand why the prediction was made and whether it is correct.

This study will take you approximately 10 minutes to complete.
